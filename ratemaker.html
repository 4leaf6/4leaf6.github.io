RateMaker

Program that calculates a single fare which includes tourist tax and commissions, aimed at simplifying billing process of hotel chains.

During my days as receptionist in the Algarve region, I faced a situation regarding tourist tax: I would forget charging or simply charge the wrong amount multiple times.
That's when I wondered if there wasn't any other simpler way of charging it.
